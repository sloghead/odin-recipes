Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are perfect for any mood, Sad? Eat a cookie. Happy? Have a cookie. Raging?? Eat the cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 lare eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with the salt
  5. Sift in flour
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Cool on wire rack and enjoy!
